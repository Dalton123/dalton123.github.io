Cityzens

Watch Manchester City Women take on Doncaster Belles in the FA Women’s Super League this Bank Holiday Monday.

There will be lots of fun activities to get involved in before the match like meeting our mascot Moonbeam and face painting. On arrival you will also be able to pick up a free match programme, teamsheet and clapbanner!

Hospitality packages are also available which include refreshments, car parking and padded seating on the halfway line in the Directors box. Call 0161 444 1894 (option 1, option 2) to book. Adults £30+VAT and Under 16s £10+VAT.

Don’t forget that after the match you can also visit Autograph Alley to meet the players!
